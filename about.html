My story

I’ve been excited about offering products that people need for as long as I can remember. I started out selling my used toys and books as a kid, and grew that passion into my teens, when I started checking what my friends and people around me needed and found ways to offer that to them.

I’ve taken on my own projects and business ideas for the past 5 years, growing into business, marketing and design. Once I found UX, I felt like it was the definition of what I was trying to do.

I’ve recently graduated from an 8-month UX immersion and a front-end dev. specialization.

I’m curious, eager to learn and excited about new skills, new ideas and new ways to solve new *and old* problems.
